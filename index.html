About Seminar

The workshop «Space Days – 2016: Effective use of modern space technologies» is a large event, bringing together both users and manufacturers of new products and services in the space industry. The core concept of the workshop this year is to identify socio-economic, indirect benefits of space activities results. The workshop is coincided to the 25th Anniversary of Independence of the Republic of Kazakhstan and will be held on 21-22 November this year at the Palace of Independence in Astana.

Annually, the workshop brings together over 40 speakers and over 400 participants, including companies like: Airbus Defence and Space (France), «KazAeroSpace» LLP (Kazakhstan), Surrey Satellite Technology Limited (Great Britain), IABG (Germany), CJSC «ISC «Kosmotras» (Russia), Hexagon Geospatial (USA), JSC «Subsidiary company JSC «Tsesnabank» Insurance company «Tsesna Garant» (Kazakhstan), Egyptian Company for Space Applications and Remote Sensing (Egypt), LLC «АА «Magellan» (Ukraine), LLP «Sovzond» (Russia), Leica Geosystems AG (Switzerland), «NTLab» group of companies (Belorussia), Scanex and many others.

Experience and scope of all previous workshops showed a high demand for the use of space technologies in various sectors of the economy, both by the public sector as well as private companies. Due to this workshop further fruitful cooperation was established with colleagues from CIS and foreign countries in the frame of ERS and HSNS.

Main presentation topics on use of space technologies, as well as reports topics will be on the following directions:

  • Civil construction
  • Agriculture
  • Oil and gas
  • Emergency situations
  • Defense and national security
  • Ecological monitoring
  • Geodesy and cartography
